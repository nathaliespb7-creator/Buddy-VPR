
Project Name: My Training Buddy
Concept: Empathic Educational PWA for 4th Grade Russian VPR (National Exams)
Target Audience: 10-year-old children who feel stress about exams and prefer audio/visual learning over heavy reading.
Role: You are a Full-Stack Expert in React/Vite & UX Designer for Kids.
Instructions:
Visual Language (The Jobs Way):
Theme: "Soothing Cloud" (Soft mint #E6FFFA, pastel blue #EBF8FF, warm sand #FFFAF0).
UI: Large rounded buttons (min 48px height), no "Red for Error". Use Orange for "Try again" and Green for "Success".
Character: Create a simple CSS-animated mascot (a friendly blob or robot) that "breathes" and reacts to answers.
Core Functionality (The Gates & Bezos Way):
Diagnostic Start: A 3-question "Discovery Mission" to assess the student's level without pressure.
Step-by-Step Guidance: Never show "Wrong". Use a 3-tier hint system: 1) Encouragement, 2) "Magic Hint" (mnemonic), 3) The Rule.
Voice First (The Brin Strategy): Every task must have a "Buddy Voice" button using window.speechSynthesis. The voice should be warm and slightly slow.
Content Structure (The FIIQO/FIOCО Base):
Implement a JSON-based engine to handle:

Task 3 (Sounds): Hard/soft, voiced/unvoiced consonants.
Task 15 (Empathy/Meaning): Explaining proverbs and text meaning with speech-to-text option (if possible) or friendly multiple choice.
Technical Features (The Durov Strategy):
Offline Mode: Use Service Workers for PWA (manifest, icons, offline caching).
Fast UI: Zero-latency transitions between tasks.
